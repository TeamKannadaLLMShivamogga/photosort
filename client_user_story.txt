
# Client User Stories & Functional Specifications
**Actor:** The Client (End User / Bride & Groom)
**Role:** Select photos, review edits, manage family data, and download assets.

---

## 1. Onboarding & Landing

### US-1.1: Secure Authentication
**As a** Client,
**I want** to access the platform via a magic link or email login without creating a password,
**So that** my entry barrier is low and secure.
*   **Visual Detail:** A split-screen login page. Left side: High-quality hero image of a wedding. Right side: Clean form with "Email Address" input and "Access Event" button.
*   **System Behavior:** Validates email against the `assignedUsers` list in the database. If new, auto-creates a user profile.

### US-1.2: Event Portfolio (Landing Page)
**As a** Client assigned to multiple events (e.g., my Wedding and my Sister's Wedding),
**I want** to see a grid of all events I have access to immediately after logging in,
**So that** I can choose which project to work on.
*   **Visual Detail (EventSelector.tsx):** A grid of cards. Each card features the event cover image, event name, date, and a "View Project" button.
*   **Visual Detail:** Large "Welcome, [Name]" header with a search bar to filter events by name.

---

## 2. Dashboard & Project Overview

### US-2.1: Project Pulse Dashboard
**As a** Client,
**I want** a central dashboard showing the real-time status of my event,
**So that** I know exactly what actions are pending from my side.
*   **Visual Detail (UserDashboard.tsx):** 
    *   **Header:** Event Name, Date, and a status pill (e.g., "Selection Open" in Blue, "Editing" in Purple).
    *   **KPI Cards:** Four large cards showing "Total Photos", "Selected Count", "Deadline", and "Payment Balance".
    *   **Coverage Breakdown:** A list showing photo counts per sub-event (e.g., "Haldi: 400", "Wedding: 1200").

### US-2.2: Financial Overview (Future Scope)
**As a** Client,
**I want** to see my total package price, amount paid, and remaining balance,
**So that** I can ensure my photographer is paid on time.
*   **Visual Detail:** A progress bar showing % paid.
*   **Interaction:** A "Pay Now" button (Future) that opens a Stripe/Razorpay modal for the remaining balance.

---

## 3. Smart Gallery Experience

### US-3.1: "View All" Chronological Mode (Feature Request)
**As a** Client,
**I want** a "View All" toggle that displays every photo in a continuous, non-paginated feed sorted by time,
**So that** I can relive the event chronologically without clicking through tabs.
*   **Visual Detail:** A toggle switch "Grouped / Linear". In Linear mode, photos load via infinite scroll.

### US-3.2: AI "Best Picks" Curation
**As a** Client,
**I want** to see a dedicated tab for "AI Best Picks",
**So that** I can see the top 10-20% of photos scored highest for focus, lighting, and smiles.
*   **Visual Detail:** Photos in this view have a gold/green `Sparkles` badge in the corner.

### US-3.3: Face-Recognition Filtering
**As a** Client,
**I want** to filter the gallery by clicking on a family member's face,
**So that** I can instantly find every photo of my parents or partner.
*   **Visual Detail:** A horizontal scrollable ribbon of circular face thumbnails at the top of the gallery.
*   **Interaction:** Clicking "Mom" filters the grid to show only photos containing Mom.

### US-3.4: Advanced Tag Filtering
**As a** Client,
**I want** to filter by specific tags (e.g., "Decor", "Candid", "Stage", "Black & White"),
**So that** I can find specific types of shots for my social media.
*   **Visual Detail:** Pill-shaped filter chips. Active chips turn Indigo; inactive are White with gray borders.

---

## 4. Selection Workflow

### US-4.1: Frictionless Selection
**As a** Client,
**I want** to select photos with a single tap/click,
**So that** I can process thousands of images quickly.
*   **Visual Detail:** 
    *   **Unselected:** Photo has a faint overlay on hover.
    *   **Selected:** Photo shrinks slightly (`scale-95`), gains a thick Indigo border, and a persistent solid Checkmark icon in the top-right.

### US-4.2: Selection Counter & Limits
**As a** Client,
**I want** to see a floating counter of my selected photos (e.g., "Selected: 150 / 200"),
**So that** I know if I'm within my package limits.
*   **Visual Detail:** A sticky footer or header badge showing the count.

### US-4.3: Submission & Locking
**As a** Client,
**I want** to submit my final selection to the photographer,
**So that** editing can commence.
*   **System Behavior:** Clicking "Submit" triggers a confirmation modal: "This will lock your selection. You cannot change it afterwards."
*   **Post-Condition:** The Gallery becomes "Read-Only". Selection toggles are disabled. Status updates to `Submitted`.

---

## 5. Review & Feedback Loop

### US-5.1: Reviewing Edits
**As a** Client,
**I want** to view a separate "Edited" tab containing only the post-processed images,
**So that** I can verify the color correction and retouching.
*   **Visual Detail:** A toggle in the gallery header: "Originals" | "Final Edits".

### US-5.2: Specific Comments
**As a** Client,
**I want** to click on an edited photo and leave a specific comment (e.g., "Remove the object in background"),
**So that** the photographer knows exactly what to fix.
*   **Visual Detail:** Clicking a photo opens a lightbox. A sidebar allows typing comments. 
*   **Status:** Photo status changes to "Changes Requested" (Amber badge).

### US-5.3: Final Approval
**As a** Client,
**I want** to mark photos as "Approved",
**So that** the project can be closed and high-res assets released.
*   **Interaction:** A "Approve All" button for bulk approval.

---

## 6. Asset Delivery & Download

### US-6.1: High-Res Download (Feature Request)
**As a** Client,
**I want** to download my photos in full resolution,
**So that** I can print them or share them.
*   **Visual Detail:** 
    *   **Single:** A download icon on the lightbox of an approved photo.
    *   **Bulk:** A "Download All (ZIP)" button on the Dashboard, available *only* after the project status is "Accepted".
*   **Security:** Links should be time-limited or signed to prevent unauthorized sharing.

### US-6.2: Social Media Ready (Web Size)
**As a** Client,
**I want** a "Download Web Size" option,
**So that** I can get a smaller file optimized for Instagram/WhatsApp without compression artifacts.

---

## 7. Album Design Studio (Future Scope)

### US-7.1: Album Preview
**As a** Client,
**I want** to view digital proofs of my wedding album pages (spreads) in the "Album" tab,
**So that** I can approve the layout before printing.
*   **Visual Detail (UserAlbum.tsx):** A flipbook-style viewer or a vertical list of spreads (Left Page + Right Page).

### US-7.2: Album Comments
**As a** Client,
**I want** to leave comments on specific album spreads (e.g., "Swap this photo with photo #124"),
**So that** the design is perfect.

---

## 8. Store & Upselling

### US-8.1: Add-on Marketplace
**As a** Client,
**I want** to view a catalog of extra services (Add-ons) offered by my photographer,
**So that** I can purchase additional items like "Parent Albums" or "Reel Edits".
*   **Visual Detail (UserAddons.tsx):** Grid of service cards with Title, Description, Price, and a "Request" button.
*   **Status:** Shows "Pending Approval" after requesting.

---

## 9. Personalization & Settings

### US-9.1: Family Mapping
**As a** Client,
**I want** to upload reference selfies for my family members in the Settings tab,
**So that** the AI can accurately identify them in the gallery.
*   **Visual Detail:** A list of family members (Mom, Dad, Sibling). Each has an "Upload Photo" button.

### US-9.2: Profile Management
**As a** Client,
**I want** to update my display name and avatar,
**So that** my profile looks personalized in the dashboard.
